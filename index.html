About the collection

Crypto Zombiess is a collection of 1000 unique, crazy and colorful zombies stored in the Ethereum blockchain. The collection includes zombies from the brainchild of our creative team as well as special editions of fictional and real world celebrities. You will find them in image format, short animations and possibly in 3D format in future editions. Don't miss the news and join our zombie community. Your support is important to us to form a great community.
